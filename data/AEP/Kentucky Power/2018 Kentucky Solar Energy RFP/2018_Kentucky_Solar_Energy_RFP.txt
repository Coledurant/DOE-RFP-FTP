RFP Title: 2018 Kentucky Solar Energy RFP
Correspondence Email: None
Important Dates:

     Issue RFP: Wednesday, October 17, 2018
     Due Date: Wednesday, May 15, 2019


Description:
Kentucky Power, an American Electric Power company, has issued a Request for Proposals (RFP) to add up to 20 megawatts (MW) of solar energy to meet growing customer interest in solar options and to diversify its electric generation mix. 
 
Proposals will be evaluated based on criteria outlined in the RFP. To qualify for consideration, projects must be located within Kentucky Power’s 20-county service territory in eastern Kentucky. Projects also must be interconnected to PJM or to Kentucky Power’s distribution system, have a minimum project size of 5 MW and be operational by Dec. 31, 2021. Projects also must use local goods and services sourced, in whole or in part, from Kentucky businesses. 
 
Proposals are due May 15, 2019. All proposal responses should be directed to the RFP manager outlined in the RFP documents.
