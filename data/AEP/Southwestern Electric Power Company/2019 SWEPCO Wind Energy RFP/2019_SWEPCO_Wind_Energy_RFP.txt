Description:
Southwestern Electric Power Company (SWEPCO), an AEP company (NYSE: AEP), is requesting proposals for up to 1,200 megawatts of additional wind energy resources to be in commercial operation by Dec. 15, 2021. Proposals must have a minimum nameplate rating of 100 megawatts and are due March 1, 2019.  
 
SWEPCO is seeking to acquire new or existing projects that qualify for at least 80 percent of the federal Production Tax Credit (PTC).  In addition, projects must be located within and interconnected to SPP areas of AR, LA, OK and TX.
